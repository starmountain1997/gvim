# msmodelslim Auto Precision Tuning

Automated search for the optimal quantization configuration to meet specific accuracy targets on Ascend NPUs.

## Overview

The Auto Precision Tuning feature automates the process of searching for and evaluating quantization configurations. It iteratively tries different settings and validates them against user-defined accuracy targets.

## Usage

### Discovery
Run `msmodelslim tune -h` to see all available arguments and combine them based on your specific requirements.

For more detailed information and advanced usage, refer to the [official Auto Precision Tuning documentation](https://msmodelslim.readthedocs.io/zh-cn/latest/zh/feature_guide/auto_precision_tuning/usage/).
